## Who we are

Interstellar Labs Ltd ("Outta Space Engineering") is a software product studio that builds technologies to help people build great businesses. We design and ship custom software, mobile apps, and web applications, and we incubate our own products for emerging markets.

- **Founded:** 2021
- **Legal entity:** Interstellar Labs Ltd (United Kingdom)
- **Team size:** 24 (engineering, design, product, growth, operations)
- **Website:** https://interstellarlabs.company
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/interstellar-labs

## Mission & values

**Mission:** Build technologies that help people build great businesses.

Our values:

- **Ship with craft.** Quality is a feature. We sweat the details users feel.
- **Customer in the room.** Every decision traces back to a real user problem.
- **Default to ownership.** Small team, big surface area — everyone owns outcomes.
- **Build for the next billion.** We design for emerging markets first: low-bandwidth, mobile-first, multi-currency.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: What does Interstellar Labs do?**
We build software products and provide custom engineering services. We run our own products (Flagship, NovaTickets, CodeWithChuka) and partner with startups and enterprises on bespoke builds.

**Q: Where are you based?**
We're remote-first with hubs in Milton Keynes (UK HQ) and San Francisco (US).

**Q: What markets do you focus on?**
Emerging markets, with a strong focus on Africa — reflected in products like Flagship (flagship.africa).

**Q: How do I get in touch about a project?**
Email **hello@interstellarlabs.company** or reach out via our LinkedIn page. Engineering partnership enquiries are reviewed weekly.

**Q: Are you hiring?**
We hire opportunistically for senior engineering, product, and design roles. See the People & Culture handbook for how we work.

**Q: How is the company structured legally?**
Interstellar Labs Ltd is incorporated in the United Kingdom. The US presence operates as a regional office.
