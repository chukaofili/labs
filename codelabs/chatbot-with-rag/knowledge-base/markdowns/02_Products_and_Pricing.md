## Billing, renewals & refunds

- **Billing cycles:** Monthly plans renew on the calendar day of signup. Annual plans renew on the anniversary date.
- **Annual discount:** Annual plans are billed once upfront and save roughly **17%** versus monthly (two months free).
- **Refund window — annual plans:** Annual subscriptions can be cancelled for a **full refund within 14 days** of purchase or renewal. After 14 days, annual plans are non-refundable but remain active until the end of the paid term.
- **Refund window — monthly plans:** Monthly plans are non-refundable; cancel any time to stop the next charge. Access continues to the end of the current cycle.
- **Transaction & ticket fees** are non-refundable once a transaction or ticket sale has been processed.
- **Downgrades** take effect at the next renewal; **upgrades** are prorated and effective immediately.
- **How to cancel or request a refund:** Email **billing@interstellarlabs.company** from your account email, or use Settings → Billing → Cancel. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.
