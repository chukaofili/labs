# Interstellar Labs — Customer Support & SLA Policy

*Internal knowledge base · Last updated: June 2026 · Document owner: Customer Support*

> Applies to Flagship and NovaTickets customers. Sample data for demo/knowledge-base purposes.

## How to reach support

- **Email:** support@interstellarlabs.company
- **In-app chat:** available on paid plans (bottom-right widget)
- **Help center:** help.interstellarlabs.company
- **Status page:** status.interstellarlabs.company (live incident updates)

Always include your account email, product (Flagship / NovaTickets), and a short description with screenshots where possible. This helps us resolve faster.

## Support hours

- **Standard support:** Monday–Friday, 08:00–18:00 UK time (GMT/BST).
- **Extended coverage:** Scale, Pro Organiser, Enterprise, and Venue plans get weekend coverage (10:00–16:00 UK time, Saturday–Sunday).
- **Public holidays:** Reduced cover on UK and US public holidays; urgent incidents still monitored.

## Response & resolution targets

Targets are measured during support hours and vary by plan and severity.

| Plan | First response | Channel |
|------|----------------|---------|
| Free / Starter | Within 2 business days | Email, help center |
| Growth / Pay-as-you-go | Within 1 business day | Email, in-app chat |
| Scale / Pro Organiser | Within 4 business hours | Priority chat + email |
| Enterprise / Venue | Within 1 business hour | Dedicated manager + phone |

### Severity levels

- **SEV-1 — Critical:** Product down or payments failing for many users. Target update cadence: every 60 minutes until resolved.
- **SEV-2 — High:** Major feature broken, no workaround. First response within plan target; daily updates.
- **SEV-3 — Normal:** Minor bug or question with a workaround.
- **SEV-4 — Low:** Cosmetic issue or feature request.

## Uptime commitment

- **Standard plans:** We target 99.5% monthly uptime (best effort).
- **Enterprise / Venue:** Contractual **99.9% monthly uptime SLA**. If we miss it, eligible customers receive service credits:
  - 99.0%–99.9%: 10% credit
  - 95.0%–98.9%: 25% credit
  - Below 95.0%: 50% credit

Credits are applied to the next invoice and must be requested within 30 days of the affected month.

## Escalation path

1. Reply to your support ticket and ask to escalate.
2. Scale/Enterprise customers: contact your success manager directly.
3. Unresolved SEV-1/SEV-2 issues are escalated to the on-call engineering lead.
4. Formal complaints: **escalations@interstellarlabs.company** — acknowledged within 1 business day.

## Data, security & privacy

- Customer data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
- We follow UK GDPR. Data subject requests: **privacy@interstellarlabs.company**, fulfilled within 30 days.
- Regular backups; point-in-time restore available on Scale and above.

## What support can and can't do

- ✅ Account, billing, configuration, bug triage, how-to guidance, incident updates.
- ✅ Refund requests routed to billing per the Products & Pricing policy.
- ❌ We can't make changes inside your account without verified authorisation.
- ❌ We can't recover funds for chargebacks initiated outside our platform.

## Service credits & refunds

Support handles incident-related **service credits**. Subscription **refunds** (including the 14-day annual refund window) are governed by the Products & Pricing document and handled by **billing@interstellarlabs.company**.
